Melia's Chocolate Chip Recipe
Courtesy of Melia Syed, a wonderful woman who birthed my boyfriend

Ingredients: 
1 cup shortening
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda 
12 ounces chocolate chips
1 cup broken nuts 

Directions:
Mix together shortening, sugar, egg and vanilla until light and fluffy. Stir together dry ingredients. Stir into creamy mix. Blend well. Add chocolate chips and nuts. 
Add to pan. 
Bake at 350 degrees for 8-11 minutes or until browned.
